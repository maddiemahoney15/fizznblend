Berry Blend

  • ½ cup strawberries (fresh or frozen)
  • ½ cup blueberries
  • ½ cup raspberries
  • ½ banana
  • ¾–1 cup milk (dairy or almond milk)
  • ½ cup vanilla Greek yogurt
  • 1 tsp honey or maple syrup
  • Ice (if using fresh fruit)

Pomegranate Banana

  • 1 cup pomegranate juice
  • ½ cup pomegranate seeds
  • ½ cup strawberries
  • ½ banana
  • ½ cup vanilla Greek yogurt
  • ½–1 cup ice

Tropical Mango Pineapple

  • 1 cup mango chunks
  • 1 cup pineapple
  • ½ banana
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • Ice
